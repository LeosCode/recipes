Hummus

4/16/15 Leo

1/2 recipe

1 15oz can garbanzo beans
1 oz (2 tbsp) peanut butter
1 oz olive oil
1 clove garlic grated fine
1 oz lemon juice
1 tsp salt
1 tsp ground cumin
fresh ground pepper

Grate the garlic into a fine paste. Put the peanut butter, lemon juice, cumin, salt, and pepper into a food processor. Process until it makes an emulsion.

Add garanzo beans and pulse until broken up fine. Process continuously and drizzle in olive oil. Add reserved bean liquid 1 tbsp at a time and allow to blend. Keep processing until smooth and light. 

Adjust seasoning.
